# Vision & Mission

**Phase 5 · Status: complete · Last updated: 2026-07-03**

## Tagline
> Discover AI trends before everyone else. Validate opportunities. Build faster.

## Mission
Turn the overwhelming, fragmented firehose of AI-ecosystem signals into **scored, trustworthy,
actionable opportunities** — so any builder, creator, or investor can decide what's worth their
time and money, and know exactly what to do next.

## Vision (3–5 years)
Become the **default intelligence layer for the AI economy** — the place people open first when
they want to understand what's changing in AI and act on it. When someone asks "is this worth
building?" or "what's the next opportunity in agents/MCP/models?", the answer comes from here.

## Why now
- AI releases have outpaced any human's ability to track them; the pain is acute and growing.
- Inference costs are falling, making deep AI-driven synthesis economically viable at scale.
- Emerging surfaces (MCP, agents, model/prompt ecosystems) are under-served and ownable today.

## What we believe (principles)
1. **Decisions over headlines.** Aggregation is commoditized; the value is the scorecard and the
   "what to do next."
2. **Explainable or it doesn't ship.** Every score cites evidence and confidence; trust is the product.
3. **Cross-source neutrality.** We synthesize across ecosystems; we're beholden to none.
4. **Action is the unit of value.** Insight that doesn't change a decision is noise.
5. **Compounding moats.** Quality (eval-gated AI), retention (workspaces/alerts), and dogfooded SEO
   compound; features don't.
6. **Legal and ethical data.** Official/licensed sources only; no ToS-violating scraping.

## Strategic pillars
- **Pillar 1 — Signal → Trend.** Best-in-class ingestion + deduplication into real trends.
- **Pillar 2 — Trend → Scorecard.** Consistent, explainable 10-dimension scoring (our core IP).
- **Pillar 3 — Scorecard → Action.** SaaS/app/content/GTM/PRD/architecture suggestions.
- **Pillar 4 — Retention & Distribution.** Workspaces, alerts, briefs, reports, API, integrations,
  referral/affiliate, dogfooded SEO.

## Non-goals (deliberately not doing)
- Being a general news site, a passive feed, or an AI-tools directory.
- ToS-violating scraping of X/LinkedIn/Google Trends.
- Enterprise-only, sales-led motion in year one (we're PLG-first).

## North-star metric
**Weekly Acted-On Opportunities** — count of users who take a tracked action (save to build,
export a plan, set an alert, create an API key) on a scored opportunity per week. It captures the
whole value chain: relevant trend → trusted score → action taken.

## Guardrail metrics
Score trust (eval pass rate + user rating), data-source compliance (100% classified), unit
economics (LLM cost per active user), retention (W4 retention, brief open rate).
