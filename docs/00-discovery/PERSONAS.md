# User Personas

**Phase 3 · Status: complete · Last updated: 2026-07-03**

Six primary personas. Each drives specific scores/features (see PRD traceability). Priority order
for MVP: **Indie Builder → Founder → Creator**, with Agency/Investor/Enterprise as expansion.

---

## P1 — "Maya", the Indie Builder / Solo Dev  ⭐ primary MVP persona
- **Context:** Ships side projects; wants the next thing to build that can actually make money.
- **Goals:** Find validated, buildable AI opportunities with low competition and clear monetization.
- **Pains:** Analysis paralysis; builds things nobody wants; can't tell hype from durable trend.
- **Most-used surfaces:** Opportunity Dashboard, Trend Detail (Difficulty/Competition/Monetization
  scores + Suggested SaaS/Extension/API/Tech-Stack/MVP), Watchlists, Daily Brief.
- **Willingness to pay:** $29/mo Pro. **Success = ** picks a project she ships and monetizes.

## P2 — "David", the Startup Founder
- **Context:** Building or pivoting an AI startup; needs market + competitor intelligence.
- **Goals:** Validate direction, understand who's winning, spot whitespace, brief investors.
- **Pains:** Competitor moves surprise him; fundraising needs market narrative; time-poor.
- **Most-used surfaces:** Competitor Dashboard, Market Intelligence, Funding Dashboard, Weekly
  Reports, Suggested GTM/Pricing/PRD, exports (PDF/deck).
- **WTP:** $99/mo Team. **Success = ** faster, better-defended strategic decisions.

## P3 — "Sofia", the AI Creator / Content Builder
- **Context:** YouTube/newsletter/X creator covering AI; needs a steady stream of fresh angles.
- **Goals:** Find trending topics early with SEO/content potential before saturation.
- **Pains:** Runs out of ideas; late to trends; guesses at what will perform.
- **Most-used surfaces:** Trend Dashboard, SEO/Creator scores, Suggested Content/Keywords/Product
  Names, Daily Brief, Alerts.
- **WTP:** $29/mo Pro. **Success = ** consistent early, high-performing content.

## P4 — "Priya", the Agency Strategist (expansion)
- **Context:** Runs AI services/consulting; needs intelligence to advise clients and pitch work.
- **Goals:** Sector trend reports, opportunity maps per client vertical, white-labelable exports.
- **Pains:** Repetitive manual research per client; needs credibility artifacts.
- **Most-used surfaces:** Reports, Market/Competitor dashboards, Team workspace, exports, API.
- **WTP:** Team/Business. **Success = ** wins and retains clients with less research overhead.

## P5 — "Marcus", the Early-Stage Investor / Scout (expansion)
- **Context:** Angel/micro-VC/scout tracking AI deal flow and emerging categories.
- **Goals:** Spot rising categories, track funding + company momentum, source deals early.
- **Pains:** Deal flow is noisy; category timing is hard; wants signal not press releases.
- **Most-used surfaces:** Funding Dashboard, AI Company/Model Tracking, Market Intelligence,
  Watchlists + Alerts on companies/categories.
- **WTP:** Business tier. **Success = ** earlier, better-informed sourcing.

## P6 — "Admin/Org Owner" (internal + enterprise buyer)
- **Context:** Manages a team/org account (agency, startup, fund).
- **Goals:** Seats, roles, billing, shared watchlists/reports, usage governance, SSO.
- **Most-used surfaces:** Organization Dashboard, Admin Panel, Billing, RBAC, audit logs.
- **WTP:** Team/Business. **Success = ** control + shared value across the team.

---

## Anti-persona (explicitly out of scope for v1)
Casual "AI news reader" wanting free headlines with no intent to build/act — served by the free
brief only; not a monetization target. We do not optimize the product for pure passive consumption.
