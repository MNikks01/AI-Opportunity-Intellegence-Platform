## Where we are

**M0–M14 complete.** The product is built, tested (194 tests green, DB tier exercised in CI),
released via Changesets, and running live. The roadmap's 27 phases are all delivered and documented, and
the retention/revenue levers (referral loop, onboarding checklist, per-org personalised digest, the 10th
source, and the Business tier) have shipped.

**Next up (M15):** moat + expansion. First slice scoped — **M15-A supply-side tracking** (models / MCP
servers / repos), designed in [ADR-0005](../adr/ADR-0005-supply-side-tracking.md) and decomposed into
B-028–B-032; it builds on the existing entity model + OFFICIAL sources, so no data-source/legality gate.
The rest (competitor/funding dashboards, browser extension) stays future scope: not committed until it
enters the "Now"
column of [BACKLOG](BACKLOG.md) with acceptance criteria (per the "no speculative features" principle in
CLAUDE.md).

**Held by design (M16):** the two major dependency migrations and Langfuse are intentionally deferred —
each carries breaking changes (Prisma 5→7 generator, TS 5→6 typecheck) or needs keys, and is tracked in
the backlog rather than forced prematurely.

## Working agreement

A milestone is "done" only when its features are implemented, tested, documented, and released — the same
bar the [ROADMAP](ROADMAP.md) applies to a phase. Update this file and the roadmap together when a
milestone lands.
